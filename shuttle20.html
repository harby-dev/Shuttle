Kayak Shuttle Planner

Sea Kayak Si

Logistics and itinerary for your paddle trip

Trip details
One kayak per paddler. All cars, paddlers, and kayaks start at the launch. Cars are shuttled empty to the landing first, then drivers return so everyone paddles together.
Timing
Paddle start time is the anchor. The itinerary works backwards to calculate the meet time, and forwards to calculate arrival at the landing and when you will be home.
